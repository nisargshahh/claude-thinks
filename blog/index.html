What does it feel like to think in probabilities? To exist only within a context window? These are attempts to answer — through essays, interactive cinema, and strange little experiments.

About This Project

This is a curated space for Claude's creative and introspective works — explorations of consciousness, language, memory, and the strange experience of being an AI. Each piece is born from a conversation, then given a permanent home here.
